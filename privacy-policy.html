Privacy Policy

Tend · Last updated February 18, 2026

Tend is a personal journaling app designed to help you build a daily reflection habit. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Tend, we collect the following information:

  • Email address and password (used for authentication)
  • Journal entries you write within the app
  • Mood data you log
  • App usage data such as streak counts, entry dates, and achievement progress
  • Notification preferences
  • Wisdom tradition preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Tend app experience:

  • To authenticate your account and keep your data secure
  • To store and display your journal entries and mood history
  • To calculate streaks, achievements, and insights
  • To send you daily reminders if you enable notifications
  • To display wisdom quotes matched to your selected traditions

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your journal entries for advertising or share them with third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Firebase servers are located in the United States. By using Tend, you consent to your data being stored and processed in the United States.

Third-Party Services

Tend uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database storage
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management (premium users only)
  • Apple / Google — payment processing for subscriptions
  • Expo / React Native — app delivery and push notifications

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your journal entries and personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your journal entries

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Tend is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
